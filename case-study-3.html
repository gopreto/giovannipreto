GOEXPP - TRAVEL APP

MOBILE APP PRODUCT DESIGN

This is my very first project as a UX/UI Designer. The challenge here was to imagine I had been hired as a product designer for a new travel startup. This startup plans to launch the app once the global pandemic ends. The goal was to build a modern day mobile app that helps people plan their next trip post-pandemic.

OVERVIEW:

  • My Role: UX/UI Designer
  • Tools: Figma, Miro, Trello and Google Workspace
  • Timeline: May 22 - 4 Weeks Project
  • Project Team: Only Me

The Problem

Travelers struggle planning their trips in advance and feel stressed when they get to their destination and don't know where to go or what to do.
